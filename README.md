# LET or Not

A patient-education website about adding a Lateral Extra-articular Tenodesis (LET) to ACL reconstruction, with an interactive, evidence-based educational tool.

Live: https://letornot.com

## What's here

- Plain-language explainers (ACL & ACL reconstruction, graft types, LET).
- An interactive questionnaire that estimates whether adding an LET may be worth discussing with your surgeon, based on the published evidence.
- A references page with links to the source literature.

## Important

This site is **educational only**. It is not medical advice, not a diagnosis, and not a substitute for evaluation by a qualified surgeon. Always discuss your situation with your own surgeon.

## Tech

Static site — plain HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. No backend. No tracking. The questionnaire runs entirely in your browser and sends nothing anywhere.

Built with [Claude Code](https://claude.com/claude-code).
